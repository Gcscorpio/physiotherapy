                    About Pure

                    About Pure

                    Pure Physiotherapy is an innovative and clinically led expert MSK physiotherapy company that offers the highest quality assessment, diagnosis and treatment of musculoskeletal conditions, ranging from back pain and knee pain to sporting injuries.

                    Founded in 2006 and now part of the Unity Health Group, we have gained extensive experience in partnering with the NHS for many years and have been working on national first contact practitioner (FCP) roles since 2016. We are now one of the biggest independent providers of FCP in the country.

                    The Four Pillars

                    Our physiotherapists are all registered with the HCPC (Health & Care Professions Council) and the CSP (Chartered Society of Physiotherapists). In 2020, the CSP celebrated 100 years of being awarded its Royal Charter. Being a chartered physiotherapist demonstrates a high level of academic achievement, training and dedication to the profession.

                    As chartered physiotherapists, we work to four pillars of practice which are the fundamental aspects of our profession. These are:

                    • Exercise, movement, and rehabilitation
                    • Manual therapy and therapeutic handling
                    • Therapeutic and diagnostic technologies
                    • Allied approaches

                    Research-Led Practice

                    Pure Physiotherapy is a clinically-led physio company. We strongly believe in the need for the best clinical practice at all times. Integrating research evidence into our daily clinical work allows us to ensure the best service to each patient. Many of our physiotherapists are involved in national and international musculoskeletal research.

                    Supporting and encouraging all of our staff’s clinical development and expertise is a fundamental part of our success. Education is such a strong part of our culture, we provide training to the NHS, universities, general public and nationwide physiotherapists integrating with external experts in providing education and development.

                    A Team of Experience

                    Our physio team is made up of highly experienced physiotherapists to make sure you receive the highest quality of care.

                    Stronger together

                    Pure Physiotherapy is part of the Unity Health Group

                    Brought together in 2017, Pure Physiotherapy, Medical and Legal Admin Services (MLAS) and Rehabilitation (Rehab) Direct make up the Unity Health Group.
